# FAQ: Outlook Email Configuration

**Q: How do I configure Outlook on my mobile device?**
A: Download Outlook from the App Store. Enter your company email. You will be redirected to the SSO page. Authenticate with MFA.

**Q: Why are my emails stuck in the Outbox?**
A: This usually happens when the mailbox quota is exceeded or the network is disconnected. Check mailbox size under File -> Info.
